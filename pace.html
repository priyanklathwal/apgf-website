PACE

Partnership for ASEAN Connectivity on Energy

What is PACE?

The Partnership for ASEAN Connectivity on Energy is a coordination platform that brings together partners supporting the ASEAN Power Grid. It provides a forum for sharing information, discussing common challenges, and aligning financing support behind priority projects.

PACE formalizes existing partnerships and facilitates structured information sharing among development partners and funders to accelerate the realization of the ASEAN Power Grid vision.

PACE Objectives

1

Alignment of Support

Consider project updates and support requests to encourage alignment of financing behind priority ASEAN Power Grid projects.

2

Progress Updates

Share updates on existing work and new financing solutions that can support utilities and project sponsors.

3

Issue Identification

Identify and discuss project-level issues of common interest and promote political support for solutions.

Who Can Join PACE?

PACE welcomes a diverse range of partners committed to supporting the ASEAN Power Grid:

Multilateral Development Banks and Development Finance Institutions

Multilateral development banks and bilateral development finance institutions providing financing and technical support.

Private Sector Institutions

Commercial banks, investors, and project developers interested in ASEAN Power Grid investment opportunities.

Public Sector Institutions

Government agencies and utilities involved in power sector development and cross-border cooperation.

Knowledge and Development Support Partners

Bilateral development agencies, research organizations, NGOs, and philanthropies providing technical assistance and capacity building.

Partner Commitments

PACE partners commit to:

  • Sharing information about their work on ASEAN Power Grid-related projects and activities (within appropriate confidentiality limits)
  • Openness to exploring partnerships and co-financing opportunities across stages of project development
  • Participating in PACE forums and contributing to reporting to ASEAN

Stages of Project Support

PACE partners contribute financing and technical support across the full project lifecycle:

1

Project Identification

Initial screening and prioritization of potential projects

2

Early Preparation

Pre-feasibility studies and initial technical assessments

3

Advanced Preparation

Full feasibility studies, detailed design, and environmental assessments

4

Structuring & Financing

Financial structuring, negotiations, and reaching financial close

Financing Solutions

PACE partners provide a range of financing instruments to support ASEAN Power Grid projects:

  • Technical assistance grants for project identification and preparation
  • Investment grants as part of project financing packages
  • Public and private sector loans for project structuring and capital expenditure
  • Equity and quasi-equity financing for project development
  • Guarantee facilities and other de-risking instruments

PACE Principles

  • Flexibility Responding to diverse needs of utilities and project developers
  • Inclusivity Incorporating a wide range of stakeholders and financing sources
  • Solutions-Oriented Promoting practical solutions tailored to specific challenges

Working Group

The ASEAN Power Grid Financing Working Group coordinates PACE activities and comprises:

  • ASEAN Secretariat
  • ASEAN Centre for Energy
  • Asian Development Bank
  • World Bank
