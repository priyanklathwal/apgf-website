A Shared Vision for Regional Energy Integration

The ASEAN Power Grid is recognized as essential to achieving the region's commitments of providing affordable and reliable power to businesses and households while decarbonizing the energy sector to achieve net zero targets.

The ASEAN Power Grid can reduce the cost of the energy transition by USD 800 billion, boost GDP and employment, increase uptake of renewable energy sources, and create a more resilient energy system for all ASEAN Member States.

Initiative Partners

How We Support the ASEAN Power Grid Vision

Financing Coordination

Aligning development partners and financiers to provide coordinated support across the project lifecycle, from identification to financial close.

Partner Collaboration

Through the Partnership for ASEAN Connectivity on Energy, bringing together multilateral banks, development finance institutions, private financiers, and development agencies.

Project Preparation

Supporting feasibility studies, technical assessments, and structuring to help ASEAN Power Grid projects reach bankability and attract investment.

Regional Integration

Promoting cross-border cooperation and power trade to enhance energy security, affordability, and the clean energy transition across ASEAN.
